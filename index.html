KIT EVOLUTION TIMELINE

Brazil 1970 - The Birth of Modern Football Kits

World Cup Winners Adidas

The iconic yellow kit with green trim that Brazil wore during their triumphant 1970 World Cup campaign in Mexico. This kit became synonymous with Jairzinho, Pel√©, and the beautiful game at its finest. The three-stripe design by Adidas set the standard for future football kits.

AC Milan 1988-89 - The Birth of Modern Sponsorship

European Cup Winners Kappa

The revolutionary red and black stripes with Mediolanum sponsorship that AC Milan wore during their dominant 1988-89 season. This kit saw the emergence of the Dutch trio (Gullit, Van Basten, Rijkaard) and marked the beginning of modern football sponsorship deals. The tight-fitting design by Kappa was a departure from traditional loose kits.

First tight-fit kit Large sponsor logo Kappa branding

Barcelona 2008-09 - The Peak of Tiki-Taka

Treble Winners Nike

The classic blaugrana stripes with UNICEF sponsorship that Barcelona wore during their historic 2008-09 treble-winning season. This kit was worn by Messi, Xavi, and Iniesta at the peak of their powers, playing Pep Guardiola's revolutionary tiki-taka football. The UNICEF sponsorship marked a shift toward social responsibility in football.
