About Iconic Threads

The story behind our passion for preserving football history

Our Story

Iconic Threads was born from a simple realization: every football kit tells a story. Whether it's Brazil's legendary yellow jersey from 1970, Manchester United's treble-winning kit from 1999, or Barcelona's tiki-taka era uniform, these threads connect us to moments that defined the beautiful game.

Our team of football historians and design enthusiasts came together with a shared mission - to preserve and celebrate the rich heritage of football through the jerseys that witnessed history unfold. From iconic World Cup victories to legendary club campaigns, we document the stories behind the shirts.

What started as a personal collection has grown into a comprehensive archive, bringing together rare photographs, detailed histories, and the cultural impact of football's most memorable kits. We believe every thread has a tale to tell.
